  • One address equals one vote.
  • Represenative(Rep) eligibility requires holding 10K+ tokens for 30 days.
  • Reps with 100K or more tokens can sponsor proposals.
  • Reps with less then 100k DGT can partially sponsor proposals.
  • Fully funded(85%) proposals gain a % bonus if proposal passes, is enacted and completed.
  • Partially funded proposals gain 2500 DGT if the proposal passes, is enacted and completed.
  • Max proposal amount = (2% current treasury) * current treasury/original treasury amount .
  • Cancelled enacted proposals are penalized 10% which go into the treasury.
  • Stakers receive 100% of the stake back if a sponsor cancels a proposal.
  • Any proposals that could be considered illegal will be vetoed.
  • Digi powers expire 180 days after launch.
  • 180 days after launch, 10 elected Digi-Senators will replace the Digi.
  • All by-laws may be changed by a vote if representives vote to do so.
