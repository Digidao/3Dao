  • Representative(Rep) status requires registering and holding 10K+ DGT for 14 days.
  • Every Rep can vote and one address equals one vote.
  • Proposal initialization requires 1M DGT (sum of all initializers DGT balance).
  • Initialization points equal the DGT balance of the holder.
  • Any Rep can add initilization points.
  • Fully funded(85%) completed proposals gain a % bonus.
  • Partially funded proposals gain 2500 DGT if the proposal is completed.
  • Max proposal amount = 2% current treasury strength .
  • If proposer cancels proposal, 10% of their stake goes to the co-sponsors.
  • Only proposer, Digi or facilitator may cancel a proposal.
  • If proposal is cancelled, co-sponsors receive 100% of their stake.
  • Any proposals that could be considered illegal will be vetoed asap.
  • Digi(creator) powers expire 180 days after launch.
  • 180 days after launch, 10 or more elected Digi-Senators will replace the Digi.
  • All by-laws may be changed by a vote if representives vote to do so.
