How 3Dao Governance works
1) A registered rep proposes an idea by calling the propose() function
1a) If the rep holds more than 1 million 3dao tokens the proposal is initialized and others reps can vote on the proposal
1b) If not, other representatives can add the weight of their own tokens to the proposal and initialize it
2) Once the proposal is initialized, representatives are allowed to vote on it
3) After the voting period ends, any rep can call the tallyProposal function which will emit the proposal result.
4) Once the proposal is tallied, the proposer may enact the proposal
4a) Consul reserves right to overide vote if no supermajority
5) Once the proposal is initialized, representatives are allowed to vote on it
